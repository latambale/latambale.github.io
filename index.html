Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really invisible during screenshare?
Yes, CloakAI uses advanced overlay technology that is not captured by screenshare tools like Zoom, Google Meet, or Teams. It is only visible to your eyes on your physical screen.
Does it work on Mac?
Currently, CloakAI is compatible with Windows machines only.
Can I get a refund if I don't use it?
No, as per our terms, all sales are final. Please check the Terms and Conditions for more details on our no-refund policy.
How fast is the support?
We offer 24/7 support. If you face any issues during your interview, our team is available to assist immediately.
Does it support coding challenges like LeetCode?
Yes, the AI is specifically trained on competitive programming problems and can assist with complex algorithms, data structures, and system design questions in real-time.
What if I have to share my "Entire Screen"?
CloakAI is designed to be overlay-proof. Even if you share your "Entire Screen" instead of just a window, the overlay remains invisible to the viewer and is only visible to you on your physical monitor.
Can I use one license on multiple laptops?
No, for security reasons, the license is locked to a single machine (HWID locked). If you need to use it on a different device, you will need to purchase a separate license.
How exactly does it listen to the interviewer?
It uses a smart "Push-to-Listen" mechanism. Manual Push-to-Listen control ensures audio is captured only when YOU choose. No continuous recording. No background listening. You simply hold the Alt key while the interviewer is asking the question. As soon as you release the key, the tool stops listening, transcribes the audio, and generates the answer on your screen.
Is there a delay in getting the answer?
The response time depends on the length of the audio captured. If you hold the Alt key for a very long time, it takes a moment to transcribe and process. To minimize delay, we recommend pressing Alt only during the core part of the question.
Is there a free trial available?
We do not offer free trials due to high server costs. However, we recommend trying the "Quick Session" (2 Hours) plan for ‚Çπ1,000 to test the capabilities before committing to a longer plan.
